Introduction to Luxury Umrah Packages Birmingham

What is Umrah?

Umrah is a pilgrimage to Mecca, undertaken by Muslims around the world. Unlike Hajj, it is not obligatory but is highly recommended and can be performed at any time of the year. The pilgrimage includes rituals such as Tawaf (circumambulation of the Kaaba) and Sa’i (walking between Safa and Marwah), which hold profound spiritual significance.
